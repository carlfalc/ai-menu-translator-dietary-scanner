Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DishSafe AI.

How accurate is the translation? ▼
Our AI achieves 99% accuracy across 50+ languages. We use advanced neural networks trained on millions of menu items and food descriptions to ensure reliable translations.
Does it work without internet? ▼
Yes! Our Pro plan includes offline mode with pre-downloaded language packs for the most popular destinations. Perfect for remote areas with poor connectivity.
How does allergen detection work? ▼
Set up your dietary profile once, and our AI will automatically scan ingredients and cooking methods to flag potential allergens. We cover over 200 common allergens and dietary restrictions.

Absolutely! Our Enterprise plan offers white-label solutions for hotels and restaurants. Include your branding and provide this as a premium service to guests.
