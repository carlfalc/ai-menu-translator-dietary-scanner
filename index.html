Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the menu translation? +
Our AI achieves 98% accuracy in menu translation, trained specifically on culinary terminology and regional variations. We continuously improve our models based on real-world usage data.
Can it detect all types of allergens? +
Yes, DishSafe detects all major allergens including nuts, dairy, gluten, shellfish, soy, and more. You can customize your dietary restrictions and preferences in your profile.
Does it work offline? +
Our Explorer and Concierge plans include offline functionality for the most common languages and allergen databases, perfect for areas with limited internet connectivity.
How do hotels integrate DishSafe? +
We provide white-label solutions with custom branding, API integration, and dedicated support. Hotels can offer this as part of their concierge services or in-room amenities.
Is my dietary information secure? +
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never share personal dietary information. Your data stays on your device and our secure servers only.
